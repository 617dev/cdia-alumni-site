About this project

The "why" and "how"

For the final project for our web development certificate program at CDIABU, we were given the task of updating the Boston University Center For Digital Imaging Arts' website for alumni. Using alumni names sourced from LinkedIn and the CDIA Design Google Group, we were able to pull profile information in order to help keep alumni in touch after graduation. This website is to be used as a networking tool, a way to connect with people on potential/ongoing projects, to find talent, and to search for alumni within certain organizations. Feedback is welcome and appreciated.
